COMPANY ACTIVITY MODEL
======================

Model: CompanyActivity
----------------------

Fields:
- company: ForeignKey(Company, related_name="activities")
- code: CharField(20)
- description: CharField(200, blank=True)
- is_primary: BooleanField(default=False)
- jurisdiction: CharField(100, blank=True)

String representation:
- __str__ = "{code} - {company.name}"
